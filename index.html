ELİF KILIÇ

Research Scientist

I am a Research Scientist at the India Energy & Climate Center (IECC) at the University of California, Berkeley. I hold a Master's degree in Environmental Engineering from UC Berkeley, where I developed the Extended Heat Index (EHI-350) metric to better measure extreme heat stress impacts on human health and labor productivity.

My research focuses on heat vulnerability in Central America and Mexico, combining climate science, data analysis, and interactive tools to help communities and policymakers understand and respond to extreme heat risks. Through the ClimaCoder collaboration, I create accessible resources that translate complex climate data into actionable insights.
