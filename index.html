About Me

I am Elif Kilic, an atmospheric scientist and climate enthusiast based in California. My research focuses on extreme heat, humidity and their impacts on vulnerable communities. Outside of academia I enjoy hiking and exploring new ways to communicate science through art and coding.

This website collects my work, collaborations and writing. Scroll through the different pages to learn more about my research projects, our joint climate blog ClimaCoder, and my writing on heat stress and vulnerability across Central America and Mexico.
