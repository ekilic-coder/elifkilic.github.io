Elif Kilic Research ClimaCoder Bio

Elif Kilic

Climate Researcher & Data Scientist

I study heat stress and climate vulnerability in Central America and Mexico, developing tools and metrics to measure extreme heat impacts on human health and labor productivity. My work combines climate science, data analysis, and interactive visualization to make complex environmental data accessible and actionable.

Through the ClimaCoder collaboration, I analyze regional heat patterns and create resources for understanding climate risk across different communities and sectors.
