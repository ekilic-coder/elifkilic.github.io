ClimaCoder

ClimaCoder is a collaborative project between my partner and me. We use our combined expertise in climate science and software engineering to develop tools and analyses that improve our understanding of climate and social systems. The site is a platform for sharing open-source code, visualizations and narratives about climate extremes.

On ClimaCoder you will find our blog Climate Crossroads, where we discuss topics such as extreme heat, environmental justice, and new computational methods. We also host notebooks and tutorials for reproducible research.

Visit our joint site: climacoder.com
